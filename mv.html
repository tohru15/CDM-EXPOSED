CORE VALUES

MISSION

To prepare students to become useful citizens, God-fearing, values-oriented, and disciplined individuals who are universally competitive professionals engaged in the noble task of uplifting the quality of life in the community.

VISION

Transform individuals into responsible citizens capable of meeting the challenges of modern times.

RESPECT

Colegio de Montalban is committed to cultivating a community with high regard for others, society, and the environment.

EXCELLENCE

Colegio de Montalban is committed to nurturing excellent professionals.

ACCOUNTABILITY

Colegio de Montalban is committed to a community of responsible and productive citizens.

PHILOSOPHY

Colegio de Montalban aims to develop students into well-rounded individuals who are prepared to be useful citizens and competitive professionals.
