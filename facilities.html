Our School Facilities

Explore the facilities that help us provide quality education and holistic development.

NEW BUILDING

The new building at Colegio de Montalban houses the information desk, faculty offices for all departments, and a spacious function hall. It also serves as the home for the students of the College of Education, providing a modern and collaborative environment for both academic and extracurricular activities.

OLD BUILDING

The Old Building at Colegio de Montalban houses the Management Information Systems (MIS) and computer laboratories, providing essential resources for students and staff. It also accommodates general services and is home to students from the Business, BSIT, and BCPE programs, offering a dynamic environment for learning and professional development.

OFFICES

The office complex at Colegio de Montalban includes key facilities such as the Clinic, Registrar's Office, and the offices of the President and Vice Presidents. It also houses essential services like the Library, Guidance Office, and Accounting Office, providing students and staff with convenient access to academic, health, and administrative support.

CANTEEN

A spacious canteen providing healthy and affordable food options.

COURT

Well-maintained courts for basketball and other sports activities.

PARKING LOT

The school parking lot is a secure, well-organized space with designated areas for vehicles, clear signage, and ample lighting, ensuring safety and convenience for students, staff, and visitors.
